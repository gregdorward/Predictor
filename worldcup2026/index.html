The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the first expanded 48-team tournament, hosted across the USA, Canada and Mexico from 11 June to 19 July. With 12 groups of four and 32 teams advancing to a new Round of 32, the format rewards depth and consistency. Host nations USA, Mexico and Canada bring unprecedented home support, while Spain, France, England and Argentina headline the outright market.

Tournament format

  • 48 teams in 12 groups
  • Hosts: USA, Canada, Mexico
  • 11 Jun 2026-19 Jul 2026
  • 32 teams advance to a new Round of 32 knockout stage
